Return Policy

Quick FAQ

  • How long do I have to return an item?
    Within 3 days of delivery, as long as the product is unworn and in original packaging.
  • Do you allow exchanges?
    Yes, free size exchange is available within 3 days. If exchange isn’t possible, a refund is issued.
  • Who pays for return shipping?
    Customers cover courier charges unless the item was damaged or wrong.
  • How do I start a return?
    Message us on Messenger with your Order ID, or fill in the form below.

We want you to love your bangles from Tinkling Tales. If something isn’t quite right, here’s how returns work:

Eligibility

  • Items may be returned within 3 days of delivery.
  • Products must be unworn and in original packaging.
  • Proof of purchase is required.

How to Return

  • Contact us on Facebook or Instagram within 3 days of receiving your order.
  • Pack the item securely in original condition.
  • Use courier service as guided by our team.

Refunds

Refunds are provided only if an exchange is not possible. Processing takes 5–7 working days.

Questions? Reach us via our Contact page.

Non-Returnable

Custom or hygiene-sensitive items may not be eligible for return. You’ll be informed at purchase.
