Bangle Size Guide

Measure the widest part of your hand (around knuckles). Match circumference to size:

  • 2.2 ≈ 6.9 cm
  • 2.4 ≈ 7.2 cm
  • 2.6 ≈ 7.5 cm
  • 2.8 ≈ 7.9 cm
  • 3.0 ≈ 8.2 cm

While measuring, please consider the bangle width and allow room for comfort.
